# Standard Contract Terms

## Section 1: Definitions

### 1.1 Parties
"Client" refers to the individual or entity purchasing services.
"Provider" refers to the service provider as identified in the contract header.

### 1.2 Term
"Contract Term" means the period specified in Section 3.1.

## Section 2: Scope of Services

### 2.1 Services Provided
Provider agrees to deliver the services as outlined in Exhibit A.

### 2.2 Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- Uptime guarantee: 99.9%
- Response time: Within 24 business hours
- Resolution time: As specified in priority matrix

## Section 3: Payment Terms

### 3.1 Fees
Client agrees to pay the fees specified in Exhibit B.

### 3.2 Payment Schedule
- Monthly billing cycle
- Payment due within 30 days of invoice date
- Late payment fee: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts

### 3.3 Taxes
All fees exclude applicable taxes. Client is responsible for all taxes except Provider's income tax.

## Section 4: Termination

### 4.1 Notice Period
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

### 4.2 Early Termination
Client may terminate early by paying 50% of remaining contract value.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if the other party:
- Breaches material terms and fails to cure within 15 days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Violates applicable laws

## Section 5: Liability

### 5.1 Limitation of Liability
Provider's total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim.

### 5.2 Exclusions
Provider is not liable for:
- Indirect or consequential damages
- Loss of profits or data
- Third-party claims

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
